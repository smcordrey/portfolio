Memberships

VisioCorp offers membership in tiers, depending on one's willingness to invest in the future.

Initiate Level

Access to preliminary research reports and minor foresight abilities.

Investigator Level

Full Future Sight Glasses access — requires one eye.

Omniscient Level

Maximum foresight abilities — requires both eyes.

All members sign a permanent consent waiver before making The Exchange.
