Assault Weapon Ban

This campaign advocates for a federal assault weapons ban by spotlighting the everyday realities students face in American schools. Through bold headlines like “Crowded Classrooms? We’ll Take That,” “Safety Sparks Curiosity,” and “Breaking Free from Lockdowns to Brighter Futures,” we challenge the normalization of lockdown drills and armed threats in learning environments. Our goal is to replace fear with opportunity—ensuring schools remain places of growth, not survival. Using emotionally resonant visuals, student-centered narratives, and fact-driven messaging, the campaign pushes for legislative change to protect the next generation.

Bumble Bee Tuna Cookbook

I created a cookbook for Bumble Bee, showcasing their tuna recipes available on their website. My goal was to make their recipes more accessible and visually engaging by compiling them into a cohesive design. Each page includes dietary restrictions, a list of ingredients—highlighting their tuna packets—and the prep and cook times for convenience. This project was all about presenting Bumble Bee's versatile products in a way that inspires home chefs while catering to their dietary needs.

Mental Health Awareness in Schools

In high school, we had multiple weeks dedicated to sex education, and one week focused on mental health. Looking back, I find it unacceptable that mental health was only discussed for a short time. Students need more comprehensive support, and teachers must be trained to recognize, and address struggles their students may face. To help, I created a poster for school hallways, explaining common mental health symptoms and providing QR codes for students to schedule appointments with a counselor or get more information. I also designed a pamphlet targeted at educators, promoting a free 3-part training course that helps teachers and school staff better understand and support students' mental health needs.

Authentically Swiss, With a Twist

For this Toblerone campaign, I wanted to bring the brand’s Swiss heritage to life with a playful twist. The tagline is 'Authentically Swiss, With a Twist.' I created a Swiss castle, built entirely out of Toblerone bars, almost like Lincoln Logs or Legos. I also incorporated a snow globe and a bear. The bear is significant because Toblerone was created in the city of Bern, and if you look closely at the logo, you’ll see a hidden bear in the design of the mountain. This campaign is all about celebrating Toblerone's roots and exploring the history behind the brand.
